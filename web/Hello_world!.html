Hello world!

This blog is going to be a collection of some of my thoughts and recollections on some of my projects. Maybe it'll be more, maybe it'll be less. This blog won't have a fixed posting schedule, and the posts themselves will be short, just quick ideas here and there.

Why?

  1. I want to get better at writing. Having ideas is nothing if I don't have an effective medium of communicating them. At the moment I don't think that I'm particularly good at writing and I would like to improve on that front.
  2. I want to write to clarify some of my thoughts and ideas. Paul Graham has a really nice post on writing as a means of thinking. Putting my ideas into words will force me to structure my thoughts in a more coherent way, benefitting me in the long run.
  3. I want to write as a means of reflection. When I finish working on a project, I would like to document what the project was, what I learnt, and what I struggled with. Not only to serve as a historical reference, but to maybe also be of benefit to others looking to build the same thing.
    Not only with projects, but also with life in general. I don't have a very good memory, so I would like to document my thoughts, opinions and feelings about things happening in my life as they happen, so that I can look back on them later on.

Thanks for reading!
